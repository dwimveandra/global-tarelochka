1.TED.com features interactive transcripts for most videos in our library. To access a transcript, click the Transcript button underneath the video player.

2.If the talk has been translated into a certain language, you'll be able to view the transcript in that language by clicking the drop-down menu and selecting it from the list.

3.These transcripts are interactive: the words are highlighted as the speaker says them, and if you click on the text, the video will skip directly to that sentence.

4.If you'd like to print the transcript, follow these steps:
Highlight the text by dragging the cursor over it
Copy the text to your clipboard
